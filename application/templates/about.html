Welcome to PleaseDontSing.com

About this Project

I am a graduating senior at Carthage College majoring in Computer Science and Data Science. I am very interested in technology and machine learning, but I also play viola in the Carthage Philharmonic and have always had a passion for music. To combine these two interests, I decided to do my senior thesis on whether it is possible to predict the "enjoyability" of a piece of music by the raw data that we can extract from an mp3 recording. In order to do this, I collected 88 recordings of "Happy Birthday" in varying skill levels, instruments, and pitches. I then collected almost 600 ratings on that data, which indicated how much the reviewers enjoyed the performance. Using this raw data, I have implemented different regression and classification models to try to best predict how "good" a recording is
